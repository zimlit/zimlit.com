part 1: eo

in this step you orient all of the edges on the cube

an edge is oriented when it can be solved with only [R, U, D, L, F2, B2] moves

To orient edges you first have to rcognize them. To recognize wich edges are oriented hold your scrambeled cube in any orientation and look at the edges. Start by looking at the edges in the E slice if any edge doesn't have the front or back colors facing out is bad. Now look at the top and bottom layers if any edge doesn't have the top or bottom colors facing out is bad.
